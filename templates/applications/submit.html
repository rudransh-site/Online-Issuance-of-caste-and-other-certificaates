{% if cert_type == 'CASTE' %}
What to upload: A caste certificate from your Gram Panchayat, Ward Officer, or previously issued school/employer certificate as proof.

First time applicant? Upload a self-declaration affidavit stating your caste and category, signed and attested by a local authority or Gazetted Officer.
{% elif cert_type == 'INCOME' %}
What to upload: Your previous year Income Tax Return (ITR) filing, salary slip, or Form 16 as income proof.

No formal income proof? Upload a self-declaration affidavit stating your annual income and occupation, signed and attested. Accepted for first-time applicants with no prior documentation.
{% elif cert_type == 'BIRTH' %}
What to upload: The birth record certificate issued by the hospital or nursing home where the birth took place.

Born at home or no hospital record? Upload a self-declaration affidavit from parents stating the date, place, and circumstances of birth, attested by a Ward Officer or local authority.
{% elif cert_type == 'DEATH' %}
What to upload: The death certificate issued by the hospital or morgue confirming the death.

No hospital or morgue record? Upload a self-declaration affidavit from next of kin stating the date, place, and cause of death, attested by a local authority or two witnesses.
{% endif %}
