Why Use This Portal?

Designed for transparency, efficiency, and accountability

Fast Processing

Strict 7-day processing rule enforced automatically. No delays, no excuses. Officers are held accountable.

Real-Time Tracking

Track your application status at every stage — from submission to approval. Full transparency guaranteed.

Apply From Home

No need to visit any government office. Fill the form online, upload your Aadhaar, and submit — all from your phone or laptop.

Simple Forms

Each certificate type has a guided, easy-to-fill form. Know exactly what information is needed before you start.

Secure & Private

Your Aadhaar and personal data are handled securely. Only authorized government officers can access your application.

Officer Remarks

If your application is rejected, the officer must provide a clear reason. No arbitrary rejections — full transparency.
